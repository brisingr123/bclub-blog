About Us

This is who we are

Business Club, Technology Students’ Gymkhana, IIT Kharagpur is IIT Kharagpur’s in-campus society for all things business.

The club's primary objective is to provide for students a platform, where they can improve their understanding of finance, consulting and data analytics; subjects with an ever-increasing importance, but insufficient exposure due to lack of guidance.

We provide for our members, and increasingly the general student community, a knowledge sharing interface to interact with their like-minded peers and also with teachers, alumni and industry professionals.

The journey to our goals is being brought about by conducting educational open house sessions, seminars with eminent personalities, regular knowledge sessions and of course, competitions for the students to test their mettle. It doesn’t just end there, we are also committed to helping the general population in and around the campus, helping us give back to the society.
